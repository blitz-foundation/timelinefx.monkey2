timelinefx:timelinefx.tlEmitter.AngleOffset
Property AngleOffset:Float
Set the angle offset or variation. Depending on the value of AngleType (tlANGLEALIGN, tlANGLERANDOM or tlANGLE_SPECIFY), this will either set the angle offset of the particle in the case of tlANGLEALIGN and tlANGLESPECIFY, or act as the range of degrees for tlANGLE_RANDOM.
