timelinefx:timelinefx.tlEffect.EndBehaviour
Property EndBehaviour:Int
Set the end behaviour of particles traversing a line If an effect if set so that particles traverse the edge of the line, then this makes the particles behave in one of 3 ways when they reach the end of the line. By passing it either of the following const they can:
  • tlEND_LOOPAROUND:* The particles will loop back round to the beginning of the line.
  • tlEND_KILL:* The particles will be killed even if they haven't reached the end of their lifetimes yet.
  • tlLET_FREE:* The particles will be free to continue on their merry way.
